Privacy Policy of Bark O Meter

Last Updated: April 4, 2025

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and profile picture to personalize your experience.

Dog Bark Data

We store your dog's bark recordings and analysis results on our secure servers to enable access across your devices.

Device Information

We collect device type and operating system to optimize app performance and troubleshoot issues.

Data Storage: Your data is stored securely on our servers to enable features like multi-device access and historical analysis.

2. How We Use Your Data

Service Provision

To provide and maintain the Bark-O-Meter service, including syncing across your devices.

Improvements

To analyze usage patterns and improve our algorithms and user experience.

Personalization

To customize your experience using your profile information and usage history.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
  • Encryption of all data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Secure authentication protocols

While we implement robust security measures, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights

Access & Control

You can access, update, or delete your personal data through the app settings or by contacting us.

Data Export

You can request an export of your data at any time.

Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our servers.
