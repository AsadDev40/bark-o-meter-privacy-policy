Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09/10/2024

This Privacy Policy outlines how Bark O Meter, Meow O Meter, and Noisy Neighbour (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile applications (the “Apps”). By downloading, accessing, or using any of the Apps, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information:

We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it to us. If you create an account, submit feedback, or contact us directly, we may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address

b. Usage Data:

We may collect non-personal information automatically when you use the Apps, including:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version)
  • App usage data (e.g., features used, frequency of use)
  • Location data (only if enabled by you)
  • Crash reports and performance data

c. Audio and Camera Access:

The Apps require access to your device’s microphone and/or camera to measure sound levels (e.g., barking, meowing, or environmental noise) and record audio or video. No recordings are stored on our servers or shared with third parties unless you choose to do so.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Apps
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To analyze and monitor usage trends and app performance
  • To respond to your inquiries or feedback

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The Apps may contain links to third-party services, or we may use third-party tools (e.g., analytics services). These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies before use.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no data transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure. You use the Apps at your own risk.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. Non-personal usage data may be retained indefinitely for analysis purposes.

6. Your Rights

As an Australian user, you have the right to:

  • Access and correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent to the collection or processing of your data

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@barkometer.com.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Apps are not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the Apps, and the “Effective Date” will be updated. Please review this policy periodically.

9. Platform-Specific Disclosures

iOS (Apple App Store):

  • Apps request microphone and camera access explicitly.
  • Data types like location and crash reports are disclosed per Apple’s App Privacy Details.
  • If iCloud is used for preferences or storage, this will be stated clearly.

Android (Google Play Store):

  • Apps disclose data collection in the Data Safety section.
  • Use of analytics or Ad SDKs (e.g., AdMob) is disclosed along with any related data collection.
